Esther Yun Kong

Esther Yun Kong is a Korean-American filmmaker based in New York. Her work explores memory, family, history, womanhood, and inherited violence through experimental, narrative, and visually driven forms.

Her films often center Korean women and reimagine historical and personal narratives through dark humor, surreal imagery, and intimate emotional worlds.
